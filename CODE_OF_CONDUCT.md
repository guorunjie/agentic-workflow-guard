# Code Of Conduct

Agentic Workflow Guard is a security project. Good contributions require directness, patience, and careful handling of sensitive details.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific in issues, reviews, and discussions.
- Assume good faith while still asking for evidence.
- Keep reports focused on reproducible behavior.
- Avoid posting secrets, private workflow exports, customer data, or exploit details in public threads.
- Credit prior work and related projects where relevant.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private security details before maintainers have a chance to respond.
- Submitting fixtures that contain real credentials or private data.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove harmful content, close issues, or block participants who repeatedly violate these expectations. Security-sensitive reports may be redirected to private vulnerability reporting.
